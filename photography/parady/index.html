John Parady
Photographer

John Parady is a professional photographer from Ft. Lauderdale, Florida. He currently attends the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida. John has worked as an photographer for the Sun-Sentinel, The Odyssey, Gator Country. He has been a three-time finalist in the PhotoUF competition at the University of Florida, a campus photography contest among students, staff and faculty (2010, 2011, 2012).
