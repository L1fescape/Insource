Sylva Kabuanseya
Photographer

Sylva Kabuanseya is a professional photographer from Miami, FL. He is a Junior at the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida. Sylva is a fast learning photographer with remarkable creativity and concepts in all areas of Photography. His current Position include, Director of Photography for 2013 Gator Growl & Homecoming and Photographer for Anatomy Promotions. Sylva has also worked as the Official Photographer for The Florida Invitational Step Show. In addition, Sylva has been assigned designated project to take conceptual driven portraits of Models, Actresses and actors from South Beach through Gainesville. In addition to taking , Campus Organizations, Graduations, Engagements, Special Events, and Family Portraitures, Sylva enjoys building relationships with each client and providing them with an amazing experience through the art of Photography.
