About

About Company

Insource University provides the link between businesses searching for technology services and local college students who can meet their needs. College students are a wealth of untapped talent that businesses can now utilize for a variety of projects.

Each project, ranging from a new logo to an updated website, provides the students valuable work experience and some cash in their wallet. While the businesses receive professional and personalized work, without breaking the bank!

The best part is that Insource University pre-screens it's students to provide businesses with quality students and great results.

Story

Scott Salzman, a University of Florida student, was working on an team in charge of one of the largest student agencies on campus. The team found themselves in need of professional web developer for a major project. Due to the lack of suitable options the team ended up outsourcing the project at a costly rate.

The tedious process of impersonal phone calls and e-mails coupled with the high expense of the project sparked Scott's intuition. Why not tap into the professional talent all around him, the students? Scott then recruited a team of experienced student entrepreneurs to bring a way to find these skilled students to reality.

Insource University now provides a marketplace with a win-win situation. Businesses can find a local cost effective solutions to their problems while students can exercise their skills while making money and providing great results.

How It Works

Artist

You have the skills all you need to do is send us your best work. Once you have been accepted to be an Insource Artist, we set up your profile! Insource University then dives into the market and promotes your work. Businesses browse your portfolios and can easily contact you for their next project. Hassle-free, worry-free, and best of all, free to showcase your talents!

Business

You have a project and we have an artist that can help. You can easily browse through any artist's work to find the style and skill sets you are looking for. Once you have found your match, making the first contact is easy with the information provided on the artist's profile. Professional and affordable service is just a few clicks away.

FAQ

For Businesses/Organizations

How do I contact an Artist?

Simply click on the artist's profile and find their work e-mail.

How do I know that my Artist is reliable?

All Insource University Artists have been pre-screened for skill and professionalism.

How do I pay the artist?

Insource University is simply the middle man in this transaction. You will pay the artist directly.

Who is liable if something goes wrong?

The business transaction is between the business and the artist. Insource University simply suggest artists to choose from.

FAQ

For Artists

How do I become an Insource Artist?

If you are interested in becoming an Insource Artist you must first send us an e-mail with your name and category(ies) you wish to be featured in to artists@insourceuniversity.com. We will then reply with the necessary information we need from you to set up your account. If selected you will be notified and be able to see your profile on the site within 72 hours.

How do I get a project?

Businesses will browse the profiles for the style and skill sets they are looking for. Once a business expresses interest in your work, they will contact us and we'll see if you're available to do the job.

How do I set my price?

You have the freedom to negotiate with the business based on the project length and amount of work required.

How do I get paid?

As the project progresses, both you and business will log the amount of hours completed. Insource University, Inc. will send the appropriate payments by check based on the terms of your agreement with the business. All payments will be distributed bi-weekly to the address you have provided.

How does Insource University make money?

Insource University, Inc. provides many services to the artist such as marketing and legal support, therefore Insource University, Inc. will receive 20% of the business transaction that is facilitated.
